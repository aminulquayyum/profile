Welcome to My Website!

Aminul Quayyum

Monitoring & Evaluation Officer, Max Foundation, Khulna

I am working as a development professional, specifically, as a "Monitoring Officer" with an international NGO, Max Foundation, in their flagship program"Max Nutri-WASH" whose goal is to reduce water and fecal-borne diseases and thus to improve child health by reducing stunting of under five children inits working area in the southern part of Bangladesh where safe & drinkable water is scarce and water salinity is high. The expected result of the projectwill directly contribute to achieve three UN- Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).These are-Goals 3, 5 and 6 - ensuring good health & wellbeing, gender equality, clean water & sanitation. Prior to that I worked in a national level NGO named, FIVDB (Friends in Village Development Bangladesh) to ensure quality nutrition services for target population of a Sub-District of Sylhet region.
